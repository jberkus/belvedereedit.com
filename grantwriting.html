Grantwriting

Grant research: Got a project, but don't know where to look? She'll find the foundations, corporation grants, and even federal grants that want to fund your project.

Grant writing: Know your project and your desired funder, but don't have the time to write the grant? Put it in her hands, and you'll have a complete, well-written grant.

Grant editing: Want another set of eyes on your grant before you submit it? She can catch what you missed — from typos to missing information.

Kris has written grants for the San Francisco Choral Society, the Prelinger Library, the Mechanics' Institue Library and Archives, and the Britt Festival.
